David Bender

Perpetual Recycling Solutions

In Richmond we found a location where we could create a state-of-the-art facility producing 85 million pounds of plastic flake. Our FDA-approved flake can be found in food and beverage containers all over the world. In Richmond, we not only found a great location, but a ready and willing workforce. The EDC of Wayne County has been a valuable resource for our business.

Tineke Veldhuis

Holland Colours Americas

We sell 800 different products to 250 customers in North and South America from our facility in Richmond, Indiana. We use a natural, renewable raw material as the principle ingredient providing plastics processors with unique coloring options for a variety of plastics resins and recycled plastics.  A quality product combined with a quality workforce creates success for Holland Colours Americas.

Gerald Davis

Vandor Corporation

We love it in Richmond, Indiana. The many opportunities here have allowed us to grow our own companies and purchase the real estate we need. We buy old buildings and build new ones and we’re always ready to move if a new business can utilize that space better. Whatever it takes to get you here! And you couldn’t ask for a better and more dedicated workforce.
